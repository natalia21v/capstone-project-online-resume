My Hobbies

Reading

I enjoy reading, especially romantic novels, as well as some science fiction and horror. I find that immersing myself in different worlds through books is both relaxing and inspiring.

Watching Movies

I love watching movies across various genres. Whether it's a classic film or the latest release, I enjoy the storytelling and the emotional experiences that movies provide.

Drawing

Drawing is a creative outlet for me. I love expressing my imagination through art and experimenting with different styles and techniques.

Video Games

Gaming is a big part of my life. I’m passionate about video games, with a particular fondness for Fortnite and Animal Crossing. My favorite genres include:

  • Shooters: Fast-paced action and strategic gameplay.
  • Resource Management: Games that challenge me to efficiently manage and allocate resources.
  • Survival: Games that test my ability to endure and thrive in challenging environments.
  • Horror: Thrilling experiences that keep me on the edge of my seat.
  • Skill-based: Games that require precision and quick reflexes.

Fashion

I have a great passion for fashion. I enjoy keeping up with the latest trends, experimenting with different styles, and expressing myself through clothing and accessories.

I find that each of these hobbies allows me to unwind, engage my creativity, and enjoy time spent both alone and with friends.
